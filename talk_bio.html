Talk Bio

Weiran Huang is an associate professor at Qing Yuan Research Institute of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. His research focuses on machine learning theory, self-supervised learning and few-shot learning. He earned his PhD in computer science from IIIS at Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Turing Award Laureate Prof. Andrew Yao and IEEE Fellow Prof. Wei Chen. Prior to that, he received his bachelor's degree from the department of electronic engineering at Tsinghua University. His work has been published in top-tier AI conferences such as ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, AISTATS, CVPR and ICCV. He also serves as a reviewer for ICML, NeurIPS and ICLR.
